How To Play

Choose the number of players that you would like to play the game. When the game starts a 10 second countdown will start when the timer hits 0, which ever play is the first to hit their key will get to choose a question and go.
Player 1 = a

Player 2 = p

Player 3 = n

Player 4 = t

The timer will reset after the question is answered corret or wrong. Once answered wrong the card will flip over, if correct the card will stay flipped and you can not flip it over again.
